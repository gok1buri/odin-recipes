Toast

Description

Toast is a perfect way to start your day or as a addition to other dishes.

For inspiration, see our recipe for Scrambled Eggs

To make toast, you'll need:

  • bread slice
  • butter or oil(optional)
  • garlic (optional)
  • cheese (optional)
Instructions
  1. Heat up a pan on a small flame
  2. Add a little bit of butter or oil
  3. When pan is well hot, put a toast on top
  4. Cook until brown
  5. Turn to the other side and repeat
  6. Optionally you can rub some garic into the bread to give it a nice taste or put a slice of chease on top.

Best served cooled in glass cups.

Enjoy!
