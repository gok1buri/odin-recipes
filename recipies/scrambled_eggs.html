Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are an easy, cheap and quick meal

To make scrambled eggs, you'll need:

  • eggs
  • butter or oil(optional)
  • salt
  • cheese or hardened cheese (optional)
  • chive
Instructions
  1. Heat up a pan on a small flame
  2. Add butter or oil - it will make the eggs stick to the pan less
  3. Cook slowly under low heat, mixing every now and then
  4. When eggs are almost cooked add salt and cheese
  5. Serve with a sprinkle of chopped chive

Best served cooled in glass cups.

Enjoy!
