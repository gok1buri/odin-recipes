Lemonade

Description

Leomande is a refreshing drink, perfect for summer parties

To make lemonade, you'll need:

  • lemons
  • water (still or sparkling)
  • sugar or honey
  • mint(optional)
  • ice(optional)
  • other fruits: strawberries, peaches, berries (optional)
Instructions
  1. Squeeze the lemons. (You can peel them first and save the lemon peel for garnish)
  2. Add sugar or honey to your liking
  3. Add water and ice or other optional ingredients

Best served cooled in glass cups.

Enjoy!
